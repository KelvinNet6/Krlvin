Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2, 2025

At KRLVIN (https://krlvin.net), we respect your privacy. This page explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect basic data automatically when you visit this website, such as:

  • IP address and browser type
  • Device information (screen size, OS, language)
  • Pages visited and time spent on each

This data is collected anonymously and cannot identify you personally.

2. Cookies

We use cookies to improve functionality and analyze site usage. Cookies are small text files stored on your device. You can choose to accept or decline cookies through our banner or your browser settings.

The cookies used on this site include:

  • Essential cookies – Required for basic site features.
  • Analytics cookies – Used via Google Tag Manager to measure site performance.

3. Google Tag Manager & Analytics

We use Google Tag Manager (GTM) to load analytics and marketing tags. GTM does not directly store personal data but may trigger scripts (like Google Analytics) that collect anonymous metrics about how you use our website.

You can learn more about how Google handles data here: Google Privacy Policy.

4. How We Use Information

  • To analyze website performance and user experience
  • To improve our content, design, and technical performance
  • To maintain website security and prevent abuse

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw cookie consent at any time

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below.

6. Data Retention

We retain analytics data for as long as necessary to improve our services. No personally identifiable data is stored on our servers.
